The Artist

Inspired by Nature.
Painting with Joy.

Courtney Salton is a Charlotte-based painter creating vibrant, nature focused works that bring life and color to any space. Her art celebrates the beauty of botanical subjects and landscapes, rendered with an energetic, joyful palette.

Whether a large floral commission or a small landscape study, each piece is created with the intention of facilitating inspired walls — artwork that uplifts, energizes, and connects viewers to the natural world.
